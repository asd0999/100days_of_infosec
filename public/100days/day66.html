CTFd —  labyrinth (part 3)

Spent my time writing what I understand is a graph solver. I programmed each node in the .data section as a dictionary having a name, a value, a left and a right direction

Example ‘a’ :

"a": {"node":"a", "val":227, "left":"c", "right":"g"},

Then I ran simulations starting with ‘a’ and continuing to jump to different nodes till the sum was greater than equal to zero. I flagged when I got a sum == 9595

The programme gave me a few solutions, which worked on the challenge. This was such a fun challenge to solve!
