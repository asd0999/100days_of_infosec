Hardware hacking

I’ve always been excited at the prospect of reverse engineer hardware. Decided to take a stab with this IoT camera I found on the junk shelf

I popped it open and explored the board. I was looking for the UART pins which are in groups of 4 - Gnd, VCC, Rx, Tx. Was easy enough to find, there was a label that said “console”, LOL

Then I soldered some wires onto to so I could interact with it

When I boot up the camera, I can see data on the UART Rx and Tx pins in the form of voltage fluctuations on the multimeter which is super cool!!!
